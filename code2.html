The Beginning

It began in the summer of 1994, not with a grand opening, but with a simple harvest. Our founders, inspired by the resilient spirit of the local farming community, sought to create a space where the distance between the earth and the plate was measured in steps, not miles.

The Epicurean was born in a refurbished grain mill, its stone walls still whispering stories of the seasons. We started with three tables and a menu that changed every morning based on what our neighbors brought to our door.

Our Philosophy

The three pillars that define our craft.

eco

Radical Seasonalism

We don't force the earth. If it's not in season, it's not on your plate. We celebrate the fleeting beauty of ramps in spring and the deep warmth of squash in winter.

handshake

Ethical Sourcing

Every purveyor is a partner. We know our farmers by name, visit their fields, and ensure that every ingredient is raised with respect for the land and the animal.

restaurant

Artisan Craft

From our 48-hour fermented sourdough to our hand-churned butter, we believe that the slow way is the only way to achieve true depth of flavor.

The Visionary

Meet Chef Julian Vance

With over twenty years in the world's most prestigious kitchens, Chef Julian Vance returned to his roots to lead SMR Hotels. His approach is one of quiet restraint—allowing the natural excellence of his ingredients to speak for themselves.
